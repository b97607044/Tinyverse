Tinyverse

Tinyverse is an open community run by a group of people contributing their skillset in conceptual art, service design, environmental engineering, Finance, Math, standup comedy, sound design, civic technology, track and field... and more. We choose to come together out of curiosity, to explore potential ways of collaboration with such a diverse group almost resembles a mini public. Interesting projects have emerged in the past six months. One of the projects got most discussed is “Currency for artists”, where we rethink the meaning of money, and work on creating an ecosystem that encourages genuine value exchange through designing a currency designed for artists and their supporters. In Tinyverse, we form projects as “Keng(坑)”s＊. It’s a space for people with interest to jump in. We are free to dig a Keng, fill it up, jump into a Keng or jump out from a Keng. We run hackathons for artists every two months, and meet up every two weeks online or offline. Tinyverse is a soon-to-be open community, if you are interested to know more, please come visit the Arthons or contact us directly. ＊ Keyboard Participation, A Case Study of “g0v.tw” on Open-source Collaborative Citizen Engagement in Hacking Community. Ting-Yu Cheng

What is ARThon
什麼是藝術松？

ARThon 是一個以藝術為主題的黑客松。藉由黑客松形式，歡迎藝術家、工程師、設計師、學者... 等不同社群的人聚集一堂，發揮所長、相互交流、完成計畫。無論是你有計畫想要找各方專業幫忙、有作品想透過跨界交流讓精緻度提升,或是好奇想看看藝術松是什麼、想在現場尋找志同道合的夥伴，都歡迎來參加！

EVENT SPIRIT
活動精神

藝術松鼓勵參與者 1. 開源、共享的循環利用(flow)藝術創作或專案執行過程中所產生的剩餘價值(surplus)、 2. 協助藝術社群具公平、共益性的基礎建設(infrastructure)建立、3. 規模化的將跨領域創作能量交換; 以期最終改善藝術環境公益及創作的永續性。
